Once signed into your account, you can change your password by clicking My Profile from the top navigation menu. Select Password and update your password.

Once signed into your account, you can update your billing information by clicking My Profile from the top navigation menu. Select Billing and update your billing information.

Once signed into your account, you can cancel your recurring subscription by clicking My Profile from the top navigation menu. Select Billing and locate your subscription plan at the bottom of the page. Click Change Plan and Cancel Membership. Your membership will be cancelled and your access will be removed at the end of your current payment period.
